# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making the BitCode community a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive place for everyone.

By participating in our community, you agree to treat others with kindness, professionalism, and respect.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive community include:

* 🤝 Being respectful and considerate.
* 💬 Giving constructive feedback.
* 🎉 Encouraging and supporting other community members.
* 🧠 Being open to learning and different perspectives.
* ❤️ Helping create a friendly and welcoming environment.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
* Personal attacks, insults, or abusive language.
* Trolling, intentional disruption, or provoking arguments.
* Sharing private information without permission.
* Spam, scams, or malicious content.
* Any behavior that makes others feel unsafe or unwelcome.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all BitCode community spaces, including:

* GitHub repositories
* GitHub Discussions
* GitHub Issues
* Pull Requests
* Community events
* Any other official BitCode platforms

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact the BitCode maintainers privately.

Please provide as much information as possible so we can investigate the situation fairly.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for maintaining a healthy community.

Depending on the severity of a violation, maintainers may:

1. Provide a friendly reminder.
2. Issue a warning.
3. Remove inappropriate content.
4. Temporarily restrict participation.
5. Permanently ban users from BitCode community spaces.

## Our Goal

BitCode exists to help people learn, create, and build amazing software together.

Let's work together to keep our community welcoming, respectful, and enjoyable for everyone.

Thank you for being part of BitCode! ❤️
